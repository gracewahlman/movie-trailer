That Thing You Do!

The one-hit Wonders make their big debut 1960's Pennsylvania and into Hollywood stardom. Laugh, sing and dance along to the iconic hits and swinging spin of this retro classic. Written and directed by Tom Hanks, That Thing You Do! is the story of five friends who make their unexpected claim to fame. A film the whole family can enjoy, That Thing You Do! will leave you wanting more and more.
